The word priority initially meant 1 thing above all others. Having multiple priorities is the same as having no priority.

I might be mixing my metaphors here, but if you plant 10 seeds and ration a cup of water over all 10 seeds, none of them sprout.But if you plant a single seed and use the entire cup to nourish the soil surrounding the seed, it will bear the most delicious of fruits.

When you cut off the excess, you cultivate the space to grow.
