Inspired by one of my favorite ideas of Stoicism illustrated most eloquently in this line:

“The impediment to action advances action. What stands in the way becomes the way.”

How you perceive the obstacles in your life defines your path forward.

The obstacle isn't a bug. It's the feature.

\textbf{Artist's Note:} This idea came to me when I was looking at my staircase. When I looked straight at them, the tiling made it look as if one stair blended into the next! It got me thinking...this is actually quite dangerous...😅
