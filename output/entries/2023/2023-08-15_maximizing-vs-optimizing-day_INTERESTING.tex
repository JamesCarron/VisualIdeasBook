Maximizing your schedule vs optimizing your schedule.

I first started thinking about this when I heard James Clear, author of “Atomic Habits,” speak on the Tim Ferris show.

He asks, when thinking about how to manage your time, \textbf{“what are you optimizing for?”}

Instead of trying to maximize your days, be a little more at peace with…well…living an optimized life unique for you.
