\textbf{The water you drink affects the ideas you think.}

As with all my visuals, there're elements of personal experience embedded in the image. For me:

Netflix - consumed me instead of me consuming it. (I felt like my watch list was a to-do list!)

TikTok - there's educational content but it's really difficult to be intentional in this format for me.

News - I have personally not been inspired to create anything after watching the news. It usually paralyzes me with despair.

We fuel or pollute our creative well with the content we consume. Curious, what fuels (or pollutes) you to be inspired to create something?
