A simple tip for communicating ideas visually: \\
Use ❌ and ✅ to communicate your point of view.

What’s the number one thing your visual metaphor should answer?

That’s right!

The \textbf{Call to Action}!

In my \textbf{maximizing vs optimizing visual}\textbf{, }I present a difference…but then…

…what am I asking the reader to consider once they’ve seen this distinction?

That’s where I can say ❌ \textbf{(avoid this)}, but instead ✅ \textbf{(do this)}!

How cool is that?! 🤓
