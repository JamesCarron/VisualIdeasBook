There’s a lot that goes into \textbf{clarifying}, \textbf{simplifying} and \textbf{amplifying} an idea and turning into a visual, but ask any creator and they’ll tell you it all starts with writing down observations.

Sounds simple enough.

But one important principle most people forget:\\
\textbf{Delay judgement }until you’re in the execution phase.

One thing I tell my students all the time is \textit{“brainstorming is an idea generation process, not a decision making one.” }
