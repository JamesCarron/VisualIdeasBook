If you don’t already have a sketchbook, consider starting one. 

\textbf{PRO TIP:} Use the same notebook to sketch and journal. Written insights inspire visual insights and vice versa.

\textbf{Law of Ideation}

\textit{Take your idea and make it real, no matter how rough. The final step is only attainable by taking the first step. }

One of my favorite creative practices is sketching visual strategies.

\textbf{What’s a visual strategy?} \\
It’s how an artist solves an idea problem with a visual solution.

For instance, the visual strategy below from the talented Wang Wei-Shen shows a possible solution to the problem: what happens when you cross a threshold.
