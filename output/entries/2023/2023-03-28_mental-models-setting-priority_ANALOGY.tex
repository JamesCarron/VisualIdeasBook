\textbf{If you don't prioritize your life someone else will.}- Gregory McKeown, author of EssentialismI've been thinking a lot about the word "priority" lately. In McKeown's book, he talks about how the word priority initially meant "one thing." The idea of multiple priorities is a modern dilemma.

I haven't landed on what my singular priority is yet, but it's something I'm working through. Coming to grips with not being able to do everything is tough, but I believe it might help me feel a bit less overwhelmed.
