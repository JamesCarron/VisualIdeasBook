\textbf{Which are you--a telephoto or wide-angle lens thinker?}

Most people want to be both (variable lens) but if forced to choose, I'd say most starting online content creators lean toward generalists.

The thing about telephoto lenses is that they're extremely difficult to focus unless you place them on a stable footing. This feels in line with how most creatives feel when they're told to niche down and specialize if they want to develop an identity to be recognized online.
