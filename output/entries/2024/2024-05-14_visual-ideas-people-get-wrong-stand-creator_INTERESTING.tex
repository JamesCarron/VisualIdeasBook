\textbf{Friendly reminder: Perfection shouldn’t even be the goal. }

This is at the heart of my creator philosophy:

One perfect circle looks just like any other perfect circle. Most people think they can’t draw because they’re comparing their drawings to what they believe it SHOULD look like.

But if you want to make stuff that stands out, it’s less important to be accurate and more important to be interesting.
