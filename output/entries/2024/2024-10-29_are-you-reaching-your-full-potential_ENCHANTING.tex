\textbf{Motion = staying busy }\\
\textbf{Action = focused execution}

I used to wear “being busy” as a badge of honor. But a lesson I’m relearning is doing a lot and doing what matters are not the same thing.

What’s worse is the busier I get, the less I focus on the latter.

Don’t make this same mistake. Create space for a few moments today to think about how you might position this week to take action on the things that matter most.
