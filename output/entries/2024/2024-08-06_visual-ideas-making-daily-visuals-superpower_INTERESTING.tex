\textbf{It gets messier before it doesn't. }

One of the most beneficial aspects of writing is that it gives your memory and thinking structure. Random events and emotions can be organized with a beginning, middle and end.

But often, I find myself creating more chaos in the process before finding the thread to tie everything together.

Ah but the satisfaction and clarity once that baggage you’ve been carrying is organized feels oh so good! 😁

“This is the only real concern of the artist, \\
to recreate out of the disorder of life \\
that order which is art.”
